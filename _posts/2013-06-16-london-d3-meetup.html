On Tuesday last week I talked at the London D3 meetup. I showed off what I've been doing with D3 to draw diagrams of graphs for presentations. I've been using my little suite of tools to draw pictures for my slide decks since last Autumn, but this was the first time that I put together a deck that consists almost entirely of graphs. It was quite a fun style and I'm sure I'll be doing it again.
